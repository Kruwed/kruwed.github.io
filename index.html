Wasabi Wallet Coinjoin Coordinator

I am coordinating coinjoin transactions with my Bitcoin node. You can view recent transaction stats from the Liquisabi or Wabisator coinjoin explorers and check the amount of inputs registered to the current round from the monitor.

How to quick connect

Copy this string and the coordinator info box will pop up in Wasabi automatically: name=Kruw&network=main&coordinatorUri=https://coinjoin.kruw.io/&readMore=https://kruw.io/&absoluteMinInputCount=21


How to manually connect

Go to the Coordinator Settings, then paste https://coinjoin.kruw.io/ and click the "Restart Wasabi" text:
