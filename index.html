Wasabi Wallet Coinjoin Coordinator

I am coordinating coinjoin transactions for users of Wasabi Wallet with my Bitcoin node. You can check the amount of inputs registered to the current round from the monitor and view recent transaction stats from the Liquisabi or Wabisator coinjoin explorers.

To quick connect in Wasabi, copy this string and the coordinator info box will pop up automatically:

name=Kruw&network=main&coordinatorUri=https://coinjoin.kruw.io/&readMore=https://kruw.io/&absoluteMinInputCount=21


How to manually connect

Go to the Coordinator Settings from the coinjoin status box, then paste https://coinjoin.kruw.io/ and click the "Restart Wasabi" text:





You can also unlock the coinjoin account on your Trezor hardware wallet. Note this should be used as an experiment only, as the feature is no longer maintained.
