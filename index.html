Wasabi Wallet Coinjoin Coordinator

I am coordinating coinjoin transactions for users of Wasabi Wallet, BTCPay Server, and Trezor (experimental). You can check the amount of inputs registered to the current round from the monitor and view recent stats from the Wasabist.io coinjoin explorer or from the Liquisabi bot on Nostr.

To quick connect in Wasabi, copy this string and the coordinator info box will pop up automatically:

name=Kruw&network=main&coordinatorUri=https://coinjoin.kruw.io/&coordinationFeeRate=0&readMore=https://kruw.io&absoluteMinInputCount=21

How to manually connect

Go to the Coordinator Settings from the coinjoin status box, then paste https://coinjoin.kruw.io/ and click the "Restart Wasabi" text:
