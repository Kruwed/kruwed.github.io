Frequently Asked Questions

I tried to recover my wallet, why is the transaction history blank?

When recovering your wallet using the 12 word seed phrase, you have to use the exact same password/passphrase that you chose when creating the original wallet. The password/passphrase acts as the "13th word" of your recovery seed.

Why is my transaction taking a long time to confirm?

Miners choose to confirm the highest bidding transactions based on their fee rate (measured in sats/vbyte). Each additional input and output included in a transaction increases its size, which requires more fees to be paid. You can add fees to speed up an unconfirmed transaction. However, unconfirmed coinjoins are very large and therefore expensive to speed up.

My privacy progress is stuck at 99%, why does it say "Insufficient funds eligible for coinjoin"?

If you see this message, your wallet contains a UTXO with a value that is too low to participate in a coinjoin. You can avoid creating small change UTXOs from sending payments by hovering over the shield icon in the transaction preview screen.
